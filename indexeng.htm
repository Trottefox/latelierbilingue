TEACHING METHODS

CUSTOMIZED CLASSES : Each class is designed according to your very specific needs and progress and not according to what a book will have you learn regardless of your personal situation. The material I use is varied and ranges from language manuals, internet sources, national and local newspapers to drawings, audio tracks and video excerpts.

IMPROVE YOUR PRONUNCIATION...RECORD OUR CLASSES : so many students seem to struggle with their pronunciation. Waste no time : it's all about getting used to the sounds right away and practising them. Recording our lessons should help you improve faster. You will then be able to listen to yourself, hear my corrections and then reproduce the correct version.

REGULAR CHECK UP... so you never lose sight of your target :I usually assess your level when we first meet and then every 4-6 lessons on average. It is of crucial importance that you should know where you're coming from, where you're going to and how much you've still got to reach your goal.

FRENCH AND EXPATRIATION : Let's be practical ! It is of uttermost importance that my students should soon be able to deal with the French and our French system by themselves. My teaching is also therefore very much inspired by all the worries and everyday life issues an expat has to sort out in France. I can also guide you in French to help you deal with a specific situation you're facing. In other words, I'm not just a teacher. I will also support you every step of the way to make your life as an expat much easier thanks to my experience and knowledge of the French.
